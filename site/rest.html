Chicken Manchuria

Chicken manchurian is a dish relished by all age groups as a starter dish at parties. Fried chicken balls cooked in a spicy sauce batter and served with steamed rice or hakka noodles

Dum Biryani

Dum Biryani is a one-pot dish of aromatic spices and delightful flavours - a result from slow cooking the ingredients in a sealed, heavy bottomed vessel for hours

Mango lassi

Mango lassi is made from yogurt, milk and mango pulp. It may be made with or without added sugar. In various parts of Canada, mango lassi is a cold drink consisting of sweetened kesar mango pulp mixed with yogurt, cream, or ice cream.
